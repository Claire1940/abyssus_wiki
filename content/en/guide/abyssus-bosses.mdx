<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Strategy for Boss Encounters">

Defeating the toughest encounters in the game requires a structured approach. Follow this step-by-step combat loop to dismantle any boss, whether playing solo or in a coordinated 4-player co-op squad [1].

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Analyze the Arena and Identify Safety Zones">
    Upon entering the boss arena, immediately identify the locations of environmental hazards, steam vents, and potential cover points. Note where health or shield recovery stations are situated, as you will need to route back to them during chaotic phases.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Bait and Read the Initial Attack Patterns">
    Do not immediately unload all of your ammunition. Spend the first 30 seconds of the fight keeping your distance, baiting out the boss's primary attacks, and identifying visual or audio cues. Every major ability has a telltale animation, such as a glowing core or a specific roar.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Coordinate Co-Op Roles and Add Management">
    If playing in co-op, assign clear roles [1]. One player should focus on clearing the smaller adds that spawn to prevent the arena from becoming overwhelmed. Another player should equip control-oriented blessings to slow the boss down, while the remaining players focus on dealing direct damage [1].
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute High-Damage Punish Phases">
    Wait for the boss to complete a major attack sequence or enter an invulnerability-break phase. Once the boss is temporarily vulnerable or recovering, unleash your primary and secondary fire modes, utilizing high-damage weapon mods and elemental synergies to melt their health bar [1].
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Co-op Positioning">
In multiplayer, do not cluster together. Bosses frequently target players with high-damage AoE attacks. Spacing yourselves out around the perimeter of the arena ensures that only one player has to dodge at any given time.
</Callout>
