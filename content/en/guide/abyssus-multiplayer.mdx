<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Mastering Elemental Blessings in Co-Op">

Throughout your expeditions, you will encounter ancient altars that offer powerful divine blessings. These blessings infuse your primary weapons, secondary fires, and abilities with elemental and mystic effects. In multiplayer, players should coordinate which elements they select to avoid redundant status effects and instead trigger devastating elemental combinations.

There are several core aspects to choose from, including Fire, Lightning, Frost, and Spirit. Stacking blessings of the same aspect unlocks increasingly powerful passive abilities, such as causing defeated enemies to spread status effects to nearby targets.

| Aspect | Primary Effect | Stacked Synergy Effect | Best Weapon Pairing |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fire** | Applies burning damage | Spreads fire to nearby enemies on death | Engine Rifle (High fire rate) |
| **Lightning** | Chains damage to targets | Increases chain count and stuns targets | Tesla Launcher (Arc multiplier) |
| **Frost** | Slows enemy movement | Freezes targets, increasing melee damage | Shotgun (Safe close-quarters) |
| **Spirit** | Grants health on kill | Spawns healing wisps for the entire team | Melee Wrench (Finishers) |

When coordinating with your team, try to have at least one player focusing on Frost to slow down fast-moving enemies like flying shark-like creatures, while another player utilizes Fire or Lightning to deal massive area-of-effect damage. The Spirit aspect is highly recommended for at least one team member, as the healing wisps generated from spirit kills provide essential sustain for the entire party during prolonged boss fights.

<Callout type="success" title="Elemental Chain Reactions">
If a teammate freezes an elite enemy with a Frost blessing, hitting that frozen target with a high-damage Fire secondary shot will trigger a shatter effect, dealing massive bonus damage. Always call out frozen targets to maximize your team's burst potential.
</Callout>
