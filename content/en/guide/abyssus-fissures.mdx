<Section icon={Map} title="Lobby Surge Fissure Locations">

The Lobby serves as your central hub, but it also contains the first set of anomalies. There are a total of eight surge fissures hidden around the Lobby. Because this is a safe zone, you do not have to worry about enemy encounters while searching. Finding and destroying all eight of these fissures unlocks the Tinker color in your Nautical Wardrobe.

To find these, you will need to explore every nook and cranny of the hub, including climbing rafters and searching secret rooms. Below is the detailed breakdown of where to find each Lobby fissure.

| Fissure ID | Location Description | Primary Landmark | Search Tip |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Fissure 1 | Near the Diving Bell | Main Lobby | Look close to the starting bell platform |
| Fissure 2 | Inside the Artefact Room | Ground Floor | Search the corners of the room |
| Fissure 3 | Above the Artefact Room | Upper Ledge | Climb the scaffolding directly above |
| Fissure 4 | Above the Firing Range | Rafters | Look up near the ceiling targets |
| Fissure 5 | Shipwreck Right Side | Right Docks | Search near the broken hull structure |
| Fissure 6 | Shipwreck Left Side | Left Docks | Inspect the debris on the left side |
| Fissure 7 | Secret Room | Hidden Wall | Interact with suspicious walls to open |
| Fissure 8 | Above the Tutorial Exit | Exit Gate | Look above the final archway before leaving |

<Callout type="warning" title="Secret Room Access">

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Zone Fissures & Navigation Rules">

Once you leave the safety of the Lobby, the rules for finding **abyssus fissures** change significantly. In the Abandoned Temple, Garden, and Sanctuary, you will find a total of 15 surge fissures per area. However, they do not spawn randomly along paths or transitions.

Instead, these fissures are strictly located inside combat encounter rooms. You will never find them in corridors, transition paths, or safe hallways. Furthermore, they do not appear in any Elite or Boss rooms, meaning you can focus entirely on survival during those major encounters.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Royal Abyss Fissures & Farming Routes">

The Royal Abyss is the final area you will need to search to complete your collection. This zone contains only six surge fissures, but they are highly hidden and located in dangerous territory. The environment here is treacherous, and the enemies hit harder than in previous zones.

To navigate this area efficiently, you should adopt a specific farming route that minimizes backtracking. Focus on the outer edges of the main chambers, particularly around the Golem arena where several fissures are tucked away behind massive stone pillars.
