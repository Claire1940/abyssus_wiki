<Section icon={Trophy} title="Unlocking the Scribe Achievement">

Unlocking the **Scribe** achievement is one of the most common points of confusion for players aiming for 100% completion in Abyssus [youtube]. The game states that you must unlock the logbook entries for all blessings and charms [youtube]. However, it does not explicitly explain the mechanics required to register these items permanently. 

To complete this monumental task, you must follow a specific sequence of actions for both relics and blessings.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Missing Charms">
    Navigate to the Relics section in your menu and select Charms [youtube]. Identify which charms are currently locked. Most of these are tied to straightforward challenges, such as accumulating 1,000 Gold or dealing specific damage types [youtube, web].
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Pick Up and Register Charms">
    Once a charm is unlocked, you must find and pick it up during an active expedition [youtube]. You do not need to finish the run with the charm equipped; simply picking it up and immediately dropping it is enough to register it in your logbook [youtube].
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Register All Blessings">
    For blessings, you must equip the specific blessing type on your primary weapon, secondary weapon, and active ability [youtube]. You must then complete at least one full run while picking up every variation within that blessing's upgrade rows [youtube].
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Relic Type | Registration Requirement | Key Tip for Fast Unlock |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Charms** | Pick up once in an expedition [youtube] | Drop the charm immediately after picking it up to save slot space [youtube] |
| **Blessings** | Equip on primary, secondary, and ability [youtube] | Complete a full run and collect all upgrade rows for that blessing [youtube] |

By systematically working through your locked charms and equipping the necessary blessings, you will satisfy the requirements for the Scribe achievement, clearing the path toward unlocking the New Monarch title [youtube].

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Run Rule">
If you manually leave an expedition before successfully completing it or dying, the run data and any newly found relics will not be written to your logbook [web].
</Callout>

Beyond tracking achievements, the **Abyssus logbook** serves as a deep analytical tool [web]. It is divided into several sub-sections that allow you to monitor your career performance and identify areas of your gameplay that require improvement [web].

### Expedition History

The Expeditions section keeps a rolling log of your completed or failed runs [web]. This section is incredibly detailed, tracking variables such as:
- Total damage dealt and taken during the run [web]
- Overall accuracy and Weakspot accuracy percentages [web]
- The exact enemy that dealt the killing blow [web]
- Equipped weapons, active blessings, and applied forge mods [web]

### Career Player Statistics

The Player Stats sub-section aggregates your data across all expeditions to compile your lifetime profile [web]. 

| Metric Category | Specific Data Tracked | Practical Application |

| **Expedition Stats** | Success rate, win streaks, and average clear times [web] | Helps measure your consistency and speedrunning progress [web] |
