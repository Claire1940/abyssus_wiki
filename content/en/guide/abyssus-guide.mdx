<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Blessings, Charms, and Ancient Forge Mods">

Your character's power scales dynamically during a run through three primary systems: Blessings, Charms, and Ancient Forge mods [web: homepage-modules-1, web: homepage-modules-2]. Together, these systems offer over 250 Charms and Blessings and over 140 Ancient Forge upgrades [web: base-info, web: homepage-modules-2].

Blessings are granted by divine entities and attach elemental or magical properties to your attacks [web: homepage-modules-2]. These include fire (burn), ice (freeze), lightning (chain arcs), ocean (tentacle summons), shadow (debuffs), and spirit effects [web: homepage-modules-2]. Stacking Blessings from the same elemental family triggers powerful status explosions and synergy effects [web: homepage-modules-2].

Charms act as passive modifiers that alter the rules of your run [web: homepage-modules-2]. They can decrease your ability cooldowns, increase your movement speed, or grant survival bonuses [web: homepage-modules-2]. Ancient Forge mods, on the other hand, directly upgrade your weapons and skills, altering how they behave rather than just increasing their raw statistics [web: homepage-modules-2].

| System Type | Primary Acquisition | Main Effect | Maximum Impact |
| Blessings | Divine Altars | Adds elemental effects to attacks | High (Build-defining) |
| Charms | Sector Rewards | Passive rule changes and utility | Medium (Stat scaling) |
| Forge Mods | Forge Stations | Alters weapon and skill behavior | High (Weapon overhaul) |
| Skill Tree | Post-run progression | Permanent character upgrades | Persistent (Meta ease) |
