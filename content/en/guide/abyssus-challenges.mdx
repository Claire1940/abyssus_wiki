<Section icon={Target} title="High-Skill Combat Milestones">

Beyond simply killing bosses, the **abyssus challenges** include several high-skill tasks that require specific conditions or extreme damage outputs. "Exterminate" requires a single hit of 9,999 damage, while "Speedster" demands you finish an entire expedition in under 30 minutes. These challenges are designed to push the limits of your build-crafting.

One of the most difficult combat feats is "Unbeatable," which requires killing an Elite enemy using only melee attacks. This necessitates a build focused on damage reduction and melee speed, as Elites in the Royal Abyss are notoriously dangerous at close range.

<Section icon={Map} title="Endgame: Deep Water and Collection">

The true endgame of **abyssus challenges** lies in the Deep Water levels and the total collection of all game assets. Deep Water Level 30 is currently the pinnacle of difficulty, representing a massive jump in enemy health and damage. Completing this level awards the "Military" skin, a mark of a true veteran.

Furthermore, the "Collector," "Scribe," and "Proficient" challenges require you to find every weapon, ability, skill, and logbook entry. This task involves significant RNG (Random Number Generation) and multiple runs to ensure every Blessing and Charm has been encountered and recorded.

| Endgame Goal | Requirement | Reward |

| **Expert** | Complete Deep Water Level 10 | Gas Mask |
| **Master of the Abyss** | Complete Deep Water Level 30 | Military |

| **The New Monarch** | Complete ALL challenges | Admiral |
