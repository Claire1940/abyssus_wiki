<Section icon={Shield} title="Abyssus Tesla Gun Stats and Core Mechanics">

The **abyssus tesla gun** stands out as one of the most versatile weapons in the current meta. Unlike traditional ballistic weapons like the Engine Rifle, the Tesla Gun fires continuous streams of electricity that automatically arc to nearby targets [abyssusbuilds.pages.dev]. This makes it an exceptional choice for clear speed, though it requires specific build setups to maximize single-target damage against bosses.

The Tesla Gun operates on a unique charge-based ammunition system. Instead of individual bullets, it discharges continuous electrical streams that automatically seek out the nearest hostile target. Once the primary beam connects, it splits into secondary arcs that strike adjacent enemies within a moderate radius. This auto-targeting nature eliminates the need for precise aiming, allowing you to focus entirely on dodging incoming projectiles and managing your positioning.
