### Finding the Smiting Spear Shrine

After defeating the second boss of the sunken kingdom, a set of stone platforms will rise from the far side of the arena, opposite to where you initially entered. This event is highly visible, as a spectral spear flies from the sky like a meteor to mark the location. 

If you miss this event during your initial run, do not worry. A portal will permanently spawn next to the healing shrine in the boss arena, allowing you to return to the upper platforms at any time. 

<Callout type="warning" title="Platforming Hazard">
The wind currents leading to the shrine require precise double-jumping and dashing. Falling off the platforms will reset your position and deplete your health pool, so take your time between jumps.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Mechanics of the Abyssus Spear">

The **abyssus spear** functions as a highly versatile utility gadget that bridges the gap between raw damage and strategic crowd management. When thrown, the spear attaches to whatever it hits—whether it is a mutated guardian or the surrounding environment. 

Once embedded, the spear emits a continuous localized aura that deals pulsing damage to all nearby enemies. The true power of this weapon lies in its manual trigger mechanic: shooting an embedded spear forces it to pulse instantly, bypassing its standard internal cooldown and rapidly triggering your equipped elemental blessings.

| Attribute | Base Value | Description |
| **Damage Type** | Surge / AoE | Deals continuous damage in a pulse radius |
| **Active Limit** | 6 Spears | Maximum active spears deployed simultaneously |
| **Pulse Interval** | 1.5 Seconds | Base frequency of continuous aura pulses |
| **Trigger Effect** | Shot Trigger | Shooting a stuck spear forces an instant pulse |
| **Base Pulse Damage** | 40 | Standard damage dealt per aura pulse |

<Callout type="info" title="Pro Tip: Internal Cooldown Bypass">
Because shooting the spear bypasses its standard internal cooldown, pairing it with a high fire-rate weapon like the Standard Rifle allows you to trigger elemental blessings faster than any other setup in the game.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Best Mods and Ancient Forge Upgrades">

To maximize the effectiveness of your spear, you must upgrade it at the Ancient Forge. These modifications alter how the spear behaves, allowing you to transition from a single-target boss melter to an absolute room-clearing powerhouse. 

By customizing your setup, you can link multiple spears together or generate devastating shockwaves that stun aggressive waves of enemies.

| Mod Name | Type | Effect Description |
| **Split Spears** | Ability Mod | Throws additional spears diagonally left and right |
| **Charged Spear** | Ability Mod | Charge to consume stacks, increasing damage and duration |
| **Environmental Spear** | Utility Mod | Stuck spears slow enemies and increase damage taken |
| **Linked Field** | DPS Mod | Creates a high-damage energy field between stuck spears |
| **Shockwave Pulse** | Crowd Control | Generates a 40-damage stun shockwave every 3 hits |

<Callout type="tip" title="Synergy Mod Alert">
Combining **Split Spears** with **Linked Field** creates a massive web of continuous energy fields. This setup is exceptionally strong for defending choke points and locking down spawns during high-loop runs.
</Callout>
