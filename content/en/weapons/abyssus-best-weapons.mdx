### Tesla Gun
The Tesla Gun is the king of room clearing. Its primary fire shoots rapid streams of electricity that automatically arc between nearby enemies, making it incredibly easy to spread status effects like shock or freeze [1]. When paired with chain-lightning blessings, a single magazine can clear an entire wave of minor enemies before they can close the distance [1].

### Brine Revolver
For single-target damage, nothing matches the Brine Revolver. It rewards precision play with massive weak-point damage multipliers [1]. During boss fights where damage windows are tight and hazards force constant movement, the Brine Revolver allows you to quickly deliver high-damage bursts before dashing to safety [1].

### Plasma Launcher
The Plasma Launcher turns clustered enemy groups into immediate history. It fires explosive plasma spheres that deal heavy area-of-effect (AOE) damage [1]. While you must manage the fire rate and avoid self-damage in close quarters, its ability to stagger elite enemies and clear spawn portals makes it an essential tool for high-difficulty runs [1].

| Brine Revolver | Critical Multiplier | Weak-Point Focus | Delivers massive single-target burst |
| Plasma Launcher | Blast Radius | Fire Burn | Ignites large groups of enemies instantly |

A-Tier and specialty weapons are highly effective when built correctly. They often require specific Ancient Forge mods or precise playstyles to match the raw power of S-Tier options, but they offer unique tactical advantages [1].

### Combat Bow
The Combat Bow features a charging mechanic that scales damage based on draw time [1]. It is highly effective for defensive playstyles, especially when paired with turret skills or control blessings that keep enemies at a distance [1]. With the right draw-speed mods, it becomes a rapid-fire precision instrument [1].

### Fish Deity
One of the more unique weapons in the game, the Fish Deity fires slow-moving, heavy-hitting projectiles that can trigger massive chain explosions [1]. It requires you to anticipate enemy movement paths, but the payoff is immense crowd control and high burst potential in tight corridors [1].

### Engine Rifle
The Engine Rifle is the default all-rounder weapon [1]. It features stable recoil, decent range, and a forgiving magazine size [1]. While it lacks the extreme specialization of the S-Tier weapons, it is the safest choice for beginners learning boss attack patterns and basic room layouts [1].

### Specialty Choices: Shotgun, Disc Thrower, and Harpoon Gun
The remaining weapons occupy niche roles in the meta [1]:
- **Shotgun**: Delivers massive short-range damage and high knockback, but forces you to play dangerously close to exploding hazards [1].
- **Disc Thrower**: Fires ricocheting discs that shred enemies in tight rooms, but loses efficiency in open boss arenas where projectiles cannot bounce [1].
- **Harpoon Gun**: Added in Update 1.2, this weapon offers extreme precision and crowd control, pinning enemies to walls, but suffers from a slow reload speed [1].
