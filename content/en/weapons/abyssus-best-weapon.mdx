### S-Tier Weapons (The Meta Carries)

#### Tesla Gun
The Tesla Gun is widely regarded as the overall **abyssus best weapon** for general play. It shoots continuous streams of electricity that automatically chain between nearby enemies. This auto-targeting nature allows you to focus purely on your movement, dashing and double-jumping to avoid incoming projectiles while maintaining maximum damage uptime. When paired with Lightning or Ocean-style Blessings, it can melt entire rooms of brine-mutated monsters in seconds.

#### Brine Revolver
For single-target damage, nothing beats the Brine Revolver. This precision weapon rewards accurate critical hits on enemy weak points. It is the premier choice for boss encounters, allowing you to bypass invulnerability phases quickly by chunking down boss health bars during small exposure windows. It scales incredibly well with Fire burn Blessings and critical-multiplier Charms.

#### Plasma Launcher
The Plasma Launcher is your go-to option for heavy area-of-effect damage. It fires slow-moving plasma spheres that detonate upon impact, dealing massive splash damage to clustered enemies. While it requires careful positioning to avoid self-damage in tight corridors, its ability to control choke points and clear massive waves makes it an indispensable asset, especially in 4-player co-op runs.

<Callout type="success" title="Top Recommendation">
If you are playing solo, choose the **Tesla Gun** for consistent room clears. If you are playing in a coordinated co-op squad, have at least one player equip the **Brine Revolver** to handle elite enemies and bosses.
</Callout>

### Scaling Your Power
To get the most out of your loadout, you must build around your weapon's strengths. For example, fast-firing weapons like the Tesla Gun benefit most from "on-hit" status effects, such as freeze or burn. Slower, high-damage weapons like the Brine Revolver or Harpoon Gun scale better with raw damage multipliers and critical hit bonuses.

The Ancient Forge allows you to customize your weapons before starting an expedition. This system provides permanent progression, letting you socket unlocked mods that carry over between runs. As you progress through the game's 70+ hand-crafted levels, unlocking these slots should be your top priority.

<Callout type="tip" title="Forge Strategy">
Prioritize unlocking reload speed and magazine capacity mods in the Ancient Forge. Minimizing your downtime between shots is the easiest way to boost your overall DPS across all weapon types.

### Synergy Mechanics
When crafting a build, always look for the "Synergy Loop." A strong run usually starts with one repeatable action, such as firing your primary weapon, and then stacks Blessings, Charms, and Forge mods that trigger from that action. For instance, pairing a fast-firing weapon with a Blessing that summons Ocean tentacles on hit creates a constant stream of defensive decoys, trivializing crowded arena fights.

<Callout type="info" title="Co-Op Coordination">
In 4-player co-op, avoid having everyone run high-damage builds. Assigning one player to a control-focused build (using freeze or shadow debuffs) makes elite encounters significantly easier for the entire squad.
</Callout>
