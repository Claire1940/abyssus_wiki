<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering Fire Modes and Mechanics">

Understanding the dual-nature of the **Abyssus shotgun** is the key to maximizing its efficiency. The weapon operates on two distinct firing modes that should be cycled based on the density and distance of your targets.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Primary Attack">
    - **High Single-Target Damage**
    - High-impact hitscan connection
    - Low fire rate for precision
    - Best for Elites and bosses
  </Card>
  <Card title="Secondary Attack">
    - **Unlimited Multi-Target Hit**
    - Invisible tracer technology
    - Hits all enemies on screen once
    - Visual projectiles are purely aesthetic
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

The Secondary fire is particularly unique. Unlike traditional shotguns where pellets spread randomly, this weapon uses a tracer system. Every enemy within the area of effect is tagged and hit simultaneously. However, you cannot hit the same enemy twice with a single Secondary blast, making it a dedicated tool for clearing screens rather than burning down a single target.

| Mechanic | Primary Fire | Secondary Fire |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Damage Type** | Concentrated Burst | Area of Effect (AoE) |
| **Targeting** | Point-and-shoot | Screen-wide Tracers |
| **Max Targets** | Single Target | Unlimited (Within AoE) |
| **Optimal Range** | Mid Range | Close Range |
| **Visual Style** | Direct Impact | Visual Pellets (Purely Visual) |

<Callout type="warning" title="Mechanical Note">
Do not be fooled by the visual pellets of the Secondary fire. The damage is calculated instantly via tracers. If an enemy is behind cover but within the AoE cone, the tracer may not connect, even if visual pellets appear to hit them.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Forge Mods and Synergy Analysis">

The true power of the **Abyssus shotgun** is unlocked through Forge Mods. These modifications allow you to tailor the weapon's behavior to your specific build, whether you want a long-range "sniper" shotgun or a rapid-fire room clearer.

| Forge Mod Name | Primary Effect | Strategy / Synergy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Automatic Trigger** | Next Secondary deals +30% damage | Cycle Primary before AoE bursts |
| **Optimized Gauge** | +50% damage to CC'd enemies | Pair with Stun/Slow abilities |
| **Automatic Action** | Next Primary deals +30% damage | Use Secondary to buff single-target hits |
| **Compact Shell** | Kill grants +100% damage next shot | Essential for chain-killing mobs |
| **Heavy Shell** | Secondary applies Slow and Weak | Best for utility and support builds |
| **Light Frame** | Momentum increases Fire Rate/Speed | Optimal for aggressive, mobile players |
| **Sawed-Off Barrel** | +50% Damage, +100% Spread | Extreme close-quarters dominance |
| **Long Barrel** | -50% Spread, +50% Weakspot Damage | Precision builds for boss fighting |

<Callout type="tip" title="Synergy Combo">
Combine **Automatic Trigger** and **Automatic Action** to create a "weaving" playstyle. By alternating between Primary and Secondary shots, you maintain a constant 30% damage buff on every single attack.
</Callout>

When selecting mods, consider your movement style. If you dash frequently, **Light Frame** is mandatory. It builds "Momentum," which significantly increases your fire rate and movement speed based on your activity level. This momentum drains over time and resets after encounters, rewarding players who stay in the heat of battle.

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Combat Strategy">

To effectively use the **Abyssus shotgun** in high-level content, you must master the rhythm of its fire modes and the management of Forge Mod buffs.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate with Secondary">
    Start an encounter by using the Secondary fire. This clears out minor enemies and, if using **Heavy Shell**, applies Slow and Weak debuffs to larger targets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Build Momentum">
    Utilize **Light Frame** by constantly moving and dashing. Watch your Momentum bar; once it peaks, your fire rate will allow you to shred through Elite health bars with the Primary fire.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Weave Attacks">
    If you have **Automatic Trigger** or **Automatic Action**, never spam one fire mode. Alternate: Primary -> Secondary -> Primary. This ensures every shot is empowered by the previous one.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute with Precision">
    Use the **Long Barrel** mod if you are facing a boss. Aim for weakspots to capitalize on the 50% damage bonus, which stacks multiplicatively with other buffs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Tactical Advice">
The **Sawed-Off Barrel** is a double-edged sword. While the 50% damage boost is massive, the 100% spread increase makes the Primary fire almost useless at mid-range. Only take this if your build includes heavy damage-reduction or shielding.
</Callout>

</Section>
