<Section icon={Target} title="Build Synergies: Blessings and Charms">

The true depth of **abyssus gameplay** lies in the synergy between Blessings, Charms, and Ancient Forge mods. With over 250 Charms and Blessings available as of Update 1.3, the number of possible combinations is staggering. A successful build usually focuses on one repeatable action and stacks effects that trigger from it.

| Element | Primary Effect | Best Synergy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fire** | Burn / Damage over time | High fire-rate weapons like Engine Rifle |
| **Ice** | Freeze / Crowd control | Precision weapons like Brine Revolver |
| **Lightning** | Arc / Chain damage | Multi-target weapons like Tesla Gun |
| **Ocean** | Tentacle summons | Ability-heavy builds and turrets |
| **Shadow** | Debuffs / Vulnerability | Co-op roles to boost team damage |
| **Spirit** | Vengeful projectiles | High-skill projectile weapons |

Charms act as modifiers that change the rules of your run. Some might reduce your ability cooldowns every time you land a critical hit, while others might grant you a shield when your health drops below a certain threshold. The Ancient Forge further refines this by allowing you to refit your weapon to match these elemental choices.

<Callout type="warning" title="Synergy Trap">

</Callout>
