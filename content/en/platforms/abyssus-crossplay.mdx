<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enable Crossplay in Settings">
    Before attempting to invite friends from other platforms, navigate to the main options menu and ensure that the crossplay setting is toggled to "Enabled." This is generally turned on by default, but verifying it prevents connection issues.

  <Step num="2" title="Create a Multiplayer Lobby">

  <Step num="3" title="Generate and Share your Lobby Code">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Join via Lobby Code">
