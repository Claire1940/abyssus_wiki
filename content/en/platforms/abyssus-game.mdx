<Section icon={Shield} title="Boss Strategies and Progression">

Abyssus features 12 unique bosses, each requiring a specific tactical approach. Progression is gated by these encounters, and mastering their patterns is essential for unlocking deeper expedition routes. Update 1.2 and 1.3 introduced "Surge" versions of familiar bosses, adding new attack patterns to keep veteran players on their toes.

### Essential Boss Tactics
- **Golemancer**: Focus on the glowing core during the "recharge" phase. Avoid the ground-slam shockwaves by jumping at the last second.
- **Kri'Su**: This boss relies on arena hazards. Keep your eyes on the floor to avoid the rising brine pools while maintaining distance.
- **Un'glu**: A movement-heavy fight. Un'glu uses area-of-effect damage rings that require precise dashing to navigate.
