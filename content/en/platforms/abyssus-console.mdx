<Section icon={Target} title="Console Beginner Progression Steps">

Navigating the challenging roguelite loop of the game can be daunting for new console players. The standard run loop requires you to pick a starting loadout, clear hand-crafted rooms that are procedurally sequenced, gather elemental blessings, defeat elite enemies, and ultimately conquer deep-sea bosses. To help you survive your first few descents, follow these essential progression steps.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Configure Your Console Settings">
    Before diving into the deep, adjust your controller sensitivity and enable crossplay in the main menu. Adjusting the deadzone settings can significantly improve your aiming precision with analog sticks during fast-paced encounters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select a Balanced Starter Loadout">
    Begin your journey with a reliable, beginner-friendly weapon like the Engine Rifle [1]. Focus on learning weapon heat mechanics and reload timing rather than chasing complex mod combinations on your first few runs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master Dash and Arena Movement">
    Mobility is your primary shield. Practice using dashes, double-jumps, and arena verticality to avoid incoming projectiles. Standing still in combat rooms will quickly turn your diver into decorative seafood.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Prioritize Core Blessings and Charms">
    During runs, select elemental blessings that complement your weapon's firing archetype. Stacking status-inflicting blessings like Ice or Lightning on rapid-fire weapons is incredibly effective for room clearance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Invest in the Meta Progression Skill Tree">
    Failed runs are not wastes of time. Use the materials gathered during your expeditions to unlock permanent upgrades in the revamped Skill Tree, boosting your health, healing efficiency, and starting resources [1].
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Movement Tip">
Always keep at least one dash charge in reserve. Many bosses use sudden, wide-area sweep attacks that are impossible to jump over, requiring a perfectly timed dash to phase through the damage frames.
</Callout>

</Section>
