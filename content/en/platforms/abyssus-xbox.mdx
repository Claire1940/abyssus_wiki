<Section icon={Target} title="Getting Started: Xbox Beginner Guide">

Starting your first expedition in the sunken kingdom requires a shift in mindset compared to traditional shooters. In **abyssus xbox**, movement is your primary form of defense. The game rewards aggressive repositioning, utilizing dashes and double-jumps to avoid area-of-effect attacks from the 36 different enemy types.

New players should focus on understanding the "run loop." Each run consists of clearing procedurally sequenced rooms, collecting blessings, and upgrading your loadout at the Ancient Forge before facing one of the 12 formidable bosses.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Choose Your Starting Loadout">
    Select your primary weapon and initial skill. For beginners on Xbox, the Engine Rifle offers a balanced heat management system that is forgiving while learning the mechanics.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master Movement Mechanics">
    The Xbox controller layout is optimized for fluidity. Practice combining dashes with jumps to maintain momentum. Staying stationary for more than a few seconds is often fatal.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prioritize Survival Blessings">
    In your first few runs, look for Blessings that offer health regeneration or defensive shields. As you become more comfortable, you can pivot to high-damage elemental builds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Interact with the Ancient Forge">
    Use the Ancient Forge stations found between combat arenas to apply mods to your weapons. These can fundamentally change how your gun behaves, such as adding explosive rounds or homing projectiles.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Controller Setup">
Consider remapping the dash button to a bumper or trigger if you prefer keeping your thumbs on the sticks at all times. This helps with the high-mobility combat required in later stages.
</Callout>
