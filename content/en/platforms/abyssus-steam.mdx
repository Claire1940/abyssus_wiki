<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Blessings, Charms, and Ancient Forge Mods">

Expeditions are shaped by divine Blessings and passive Charms. With over 250 Charms and Blessings shared across the pool, no two runs feel identical. Blessings apply elemental or mystical effects directly to your primary fire, secondary fire, and active abilities, while Charms modify your character's baseline behavior, cooldowns, and survivability.

The Ancient Forge allows you to refit weapons and abilities mid-run, adapting your gear to the Blessings you have accumulated. Update 1.3 expanded this system by introducing a new Brine God, bringing 15 exclusive Blessings alongside 5 new Blessings for each of the existing deities.

| Element / God | Primary Effect | Best Weapon Match | Build Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fire** | Damage over time (Burn) | Brine Revolver, Engine Rifle | Stack burn stacks on high-health targets |
| **Ice** | Crowd control (Freeze) | Shotgun, Combat Bow | Freeze elite enemies to create safe windows |
| **Lightning** | Chain damage (Arc) | Tesla Gun, Disc Thrower | Maximize area damage in packed arenas |
| **Ocean** | Minion support (Summons) | Combat Bow, Plasma Launcher | Deploy tentacles to distract and damage foes |
| **Shadow** | Vulnerability (Debuffs) | Harpoon Gun, Engine Rifle | Apply debuffs before initiating heavy attacks |
| **Spirits** | Automated tracking (Projectiles)| Fish Deity, Brine Revolver | Let homing spirits clear stragglers |

By combining these elements with passive Charms (such as ability cooldown reduction or shield recovery on kill), you can specialize in dedicated roles. This is especially useful in co-op, where one player can focus on ice-based control while another focuses on fire-based damage.

<Callout type="success" title="The Golden Synergy Loop">
The most effective builds utilize a simple loop: select a core action (like your secondary fire), attach a status-applying Blessing, equip a Charm that triggers on that specific status, and use Ancient Forge mods to reduce that action's cooldown.
</Callout>
