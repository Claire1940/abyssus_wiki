<Section icon={Target} title="The Three-Step Forge Mod Loop for Infinite Turrets">

To transform a standard deployable into an unstoppable, infinite automated army, you must manipulate the game's cooldown math. By default, the Turret stays active for 20 seconds before self-destructing, but it is bound by a lengthy cooldown. By slotting specific Ancient Forge mods, you can reduce this cooldown to absolute zero. 

This loop relies on a precise combination of three ability modifications. Once fully assembled, each turret you deploy actively reduces the cooldown of your next charge, creating a self-sustaining cycle of infinite deployments.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip Extra Pockets">
    Acquire and slot the **Extra Pockets** Forge mod. This grants you +3 Ability Stacks, giving you the initial charges needed to kickstart the infinite loop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Apply Buddy System">
    Equip the **Buddy System** mod. This lowers your base Turret cooldown by 10 seconds. While it slightly reduces individual turret damage, it boosts the damage of all active turrets based on how many are nearby.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Slot Rich Get Richer">
    Add the **Rich Get Richer** mod, which decreases your remaining ability cooldown by 10% for every active stack. Alternatively, equip the **Freebie** Charm to bypass cooldown checks entirely on trigger.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Progression Check">
Because these specific mods are highly sought after, you will need to reach the third major area (Act 3) and gather enough Soul Shards to reroll your modifications at the Ancient Forge.
</Callout>
