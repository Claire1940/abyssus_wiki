<Section icon={Swords} title="Weapon Overview & The Heat Mechanic">

The Engine Rifle is the foundational automatic firearm of your Brinehunter arsenal. Unlike traditional shooters, weapons in this brinepunk world do not utilize conventional ammunition pools. Instead, the Engine Rifle operates on a thermodynamic heat system. Firing the weapon generates heat, and allowing the meter to max out triggers a temporary overheat phase, locking you out of your primary offensive capabilities. 

To build a successful loadout, you must learn to balance continuous fire with heat dissipation. Alternatively, advanced builds can leverage the overheat state for high-risk, high-reward playstyles. Understanding the baseline behavior of the weapon is crucial before diving into advanced modifications.

| Metric | Base Value | Practical Gameplay Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ammo Consumption** | None | Unlimited firing capacity governed only by heat generation |
| **Fire Rate** | Medium-High | Perfect for applying rapid status effects and continuous pressure |
| **Optimal Range** | Mid-Range | High accuracy at distance, but spread increases during sustained fire |
| **Heat Dissipation** | Moderate | Requires brief pauses or specific passive charms to cool down |

<Callout type="warning" title="Heat Warning">

<Section icon={Trophy} title="How to Unlock Engine Rifle Mods">

To unlock the full potential of your **abyssus engine rifle build**, you must complete specific in-run challenges to earn the weapon's secondary modifications. Unlocking these mods early in your progression is highly recommended to make deeper expeditions much easier.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock Gatling Fire Mode">
    Defeat 15 adversaries during a single expedition without reloading your weapon. This is easiest to achieve in early combat rooms where weak, low-health enemies spawn in clusters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock Split Shot Secondary">
    Defeat 30 enemies using explosive damage while you have the Engine Rifle equipped. Utilizing your Frag Grenade ability in crowded choke points is the fastest way to complete this objective.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Concentrated Shot">
    Eliminate 5 enemies while your Engine Rifle is fully overheated. To do this safely, apply a freeze effect to a group of enemies, fire away from them to overheat your weapon, and then finish them off while overheated.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Modification | Unlock Achievement | Easiest Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gatling Fire** | Sustained Hunter | Use precision single shots on early-game drone enemies |
| **Split Shot** | Drum Fire | Group enemies together and deploy a well-timed Frag Grenade |
| **Concentrated Shot** | Deadline | Freeze a group of enemies, overheat your weapon, and finish them |

<Callout type="warning" title="Achievement Tracking">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Strategy & Solo vs Co-op Play">
