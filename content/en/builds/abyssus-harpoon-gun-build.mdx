For a Harpoon Gun build, you want to focus on "One-Shot" synergies—effects that trigger or scale based on a single, high-damage hit rather than rapid-fire triggers.

**Top Blessing Choices for Harpoon Users:**
- **Ice (Brinefrost):** Since the Harpoon Gun hits hard, applying a massive Freeze stack in one shot allows you to safely reload while the enemy is shattered.

- **Lightning:** While usually for fast weapons, certain Lightning blessings chain damage from the point of impact, helping the Harpoon Gun with its weakness against swarms.
